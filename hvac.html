Heat & Air Conditioning

The Ecobee thermostats (one on each floor) auto-select heating or cooling based on house temperature. Use the touchscreen — tap a temperature and swipe up or down to adjust. The upstairs thermostat is in the Master Bedroom.

Don't forget the ceiling fans — they help a lot! (Light switch must be on for the fan to work; then use the remote.)

Call Eileen or Jeff with questions — we can control the thermostats remotely.

  • Emergency shutoff for lower unit: Red switch plate in the storage area on the left rear wall.
  • Emergency shutoff for upper unit: Red switch plate in the 3rd floor utility closet on the right.
