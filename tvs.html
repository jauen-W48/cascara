TVs, Streaming & Cable

All TVs can be operated with the Apple remote (small silver remote). Press the power button on the top right — after 10 seconds or so, select the Guest profile.

All streaming services are via Apple TV — not Comcast or the TV's own OS. There is no cable TV subscription.

For cable, broadcast, and local programming use YouTube TV.
