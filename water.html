Water & Plumbing

All valves are 1/4-turn: handle parallel to pipe = ON  |  handle perpendicular = OFF

Storage Area — Main Valves

ValveControlsNormal State
#1Outdoor spigots on front decks (power washing)OFF in season
#2Main house water — ON arriving, OFF departing. Also the emergency leak shutoff!ON in season
#3 & #4Recirculation pumpLeave ON
Front deck spigots are left open in winter to avoid freezing. They must be closed before turning Valve #1 on.

The yellow hose is the gas line with a red handle — only turn off in an emergency.

Other Valves

Under master bedroom sink: Controls kitchen ice maker. ON in season, OFF otherwise.

Under cabana sink: Controls water to the cabana ice maker. ON in season.

Under kitchen sink: Controls outdoor spigots on rear decks and porch. Normally OFF. Same winter warning applies.

1st floor (bunk room) bathroom: Controls outdoor shower. ON in season. Spigots left open in winter — must close before turning on.

We can control whole house water remotely. There is an emergency shutoff if a leak is detected or if an excessive amount of water is being used. Please call if there are any issues.
