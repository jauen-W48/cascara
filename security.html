Doors & Security

  • The front door key is on the rack inside the storage area (wall to the right).
  • The keypad near the front door is not reliable — please use the key.
  • Call Eileen (410-215-1752) or Jeff (443-847-6373) if needed — we can unlock the door remotely.

Cabana

Keep the sliding door into the cabana closed to keep the AC inside the house. The roll-up door opens by twisting the key and pushing up. Jiggle to lock fully — the salt air makes the key stick a bit.

🚫 NO GLASS IN THE POOL AREA. Use plastic cups or cans. Please let us know about any broken glass in or near the pool.
