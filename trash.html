Trash & Recycling

♻️ Recycling (large blue cans)

Single stream — paper, plastic, and glass all go in. No trash bags allowed. All recycling must be emptied loose from bags directly into the bin.

🗑️ Trash (large green cans)

Other waste goes in the green cans. Trash bags are fine here.

Wheel cans to the curb on Sunday and Wednesday nights.
In season: pickup Thursday morning & Monday morning.
Off season: pickup Monday morning only.
