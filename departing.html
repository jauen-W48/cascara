Departing Checklist

  • All windows and doors closed and locked
  • All perishables trashed or taken. Please don't leave anything in the fridge or pantry.
  • Beds stripped — clean sheets are in room closets. Place used sheets in hamper in the middle of your room. If pressed for time, let Eileen know.
  • Coffee maker cleaned (empty the grounds)
  • All lights off
  • Bikes and beach chairs put away, storage area closed
  • Exterior elevator key returned to the key rack inside the storage area (right wall)
  • Sign the guest book! So fun to look back on. 😊
  • Trash and recycling in the large cans. Recycling must be loose — no plastic bags. Cans to the curb on the grass.
  • Grill cleaned and covered. Gas valve turned off.
  • Cover on the back deck furniture. Corner straps under furniture feet.
  • Pool robot installed
  • Pool heater off
  • Awnings retracted — front and rear
  • Umbrellas retracted. Pool umbrella removed and laid under the deck near the hammock.
  • Tabletop fireplace moved inside
  • Pool cushions in the large white storage box (the latch is tricky)
  • Pool inflatables stored in the outdoor shower or storage area
  • Pool toys in the brown pool box
  • Cabana area and blender cleaned, cabana dishwasher run
  • Kitchen dishwasher run
  • Fireplace off and gas valve closed (gold key on the wall to the right)
  • Main water valve turned OFF in the storage area (see Water & Plumbing)
