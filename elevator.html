Elevator

Please do not let children play in the elevator.
  • Level 1 — Outside on the driveway
  • Level 2 — 1st floor (Pool / Cabana / Laundry)
  • Level 3 — Main floor (Kitchen, Living Room, Dining Room)
  • Level 4 — Upstairs bedrooms

The outside entrance is operated by turning and releasing the key in the lock. The key is on the rack in the storage area (code 24671 ENTER), wall to the right.

Shut BOTH the accordion door AND the outside door every single time you use the elevator! All doors have sensors — the elevator will not move unless every door is fully closed. It's a real bummer to arrive and find it not working because someone forgot this.

Troubleshooting

Not coming when called? Check all levels for an unclosed door. You must hit the call button or turn the outside key for the elevator door to unlock.

Stuck inside? Release at upper left — slide accordion door open and lift the small silver lever while turning the door handle. The keypad is a landline in case of emergency: (609) 830-3038.

All doors closed, still not responding? Pull the black handle on the control unit in the storage area down, wait 20 seconds, push it back up. Takes a few minutes to reset. Last resort only.
