Awnings, Curtains & Porch Swing

Bedroom Awnings (front balconies, 3rd floor — manual)

Operated manually using the cranks in each bedroom closet. Hook the crank to the labeled "HOOK HERE" point only. Do not open past the railing. Do not connect to the other hooks.

Rear Deck Awning (2nd floor — motorized)

Use the wall switch to the left. It will stop automatically. Do not use in high winds.

Always close all awnings when going to bed or leaving the house, in case a wind storm develops.

Deck Furniture Cover

The cover is in the small box next to the sideboard in the dining room. Push the table snugly against the L of the sectional and slide the bench and stool close so the cover fits. Corner straps go under the furniture feet so it doesn't blow off.

Please make sure the cover is on and secured when you leave.

Curtains

The front porch curtains can be pulled closed to shield from the afternoon sun. Tie them back when done.

Porch Swing Secret 🤫

There is a white board under the cushion that extends to support your feet when lying down. Use a long pillow on the board and stick your feet through the armrest. There is also a small rope ring near the swing — tug it gently to keep the swing moving while you relax.
