Pool

🚫 NO GLASS IN THE POOL AREA. Use plastic cups or cans only. Plastic goes on the TOP RACK of the dishwasher only.
  • Pool/beach towels are in the closet in the pool bathroom (next to the laundry room).
  • Inflatable toys: outdoor shower or storage area. Smaller toys: brown box near the spiral stairs.
  • The pool robot cleans at 10AM daily. Remove by turning the hose fitting 1/4 turn. Store under the spiral stairs when not in use.
  • Reinstall the robot every night or by 9:30AM — the pump can burn out without it.
  • The pool heater is the brown box near the AC units on the right side of the house. Press MODE until the "Pool" light illuminates, then set temperature. 82° recommended. Takes ~2 hours to warm. Turn off when not in use.
  • The pool umbrella can go in the socket at the shallow shelf. Remove at night so the robot doesn't tangle.
  • Pool chairs can go in the water on the shallow shelf under the umbrella.
  • Skimmers and pumps run on automatic timers. Pool service comes weekly.
  • Keep the inside sliding door to the cabana closed so the house AC stays inside.
  • Please turn off the pool shower valves completely (tighter than you think). They tend to leak.
