Grill

  • Pull the grill away from the house before use — the hot lid must not contact the siding or railing.
  • Wipe up dripped grease and BBQ sauce from the deck surface immediately.
  • Use the grill brush (in the compartment underneath) to clean the grates after cooking.
  • Wipe down the outside before putting on the cover (after it cools).
  • There is no propane tank. Turn on the gas wall valve behind the grill. Remember to turn it off when finished.
  • Check the small grease pan inside the doors — empty if overflowing. Disposable replacement pans are nearby.
🧯 Fire extinguisher is under the kitchen sink.
